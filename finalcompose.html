About Us

Tech Brigades work on early diagnosis of Hypoglycemia which is a condition in which your blood sugar (glucose) level is lower than the standard range. Hypoglycemia is often related to diabetes treatment. But other drugs and a variety of conditions can cause low blood sugar in people who don't have diabetes.

We are a team of 5 members whose aim is to create a model which will help in decreasing the number of deaths caused by Hypoglucemia.
